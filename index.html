Founder & vision

Who’s behind IgniteGrapple?

IgniteGrapple is built by someone who actually lived the grind — low-income background, late-blooming wrestler, state champ, and first NCAA All-American for his college.

Founder

Kevin Almond, MBA – Founder & Builder of IgniteGrapple

Kevin grew up without traditional resources — no private rooms, no expensive camps, using carpet patterns and yard space to drill motion and stance. He started wrestling JV in 8th grade, made the state finals from 10th–12th grade, and became a state champion his senior year.

In college, he became the first NCAA All-American in school history for Emmanuel College and the first All-American across all sports once the program was accepted into NCAA D2. He was later inducted into a wrestling hall of fame organization for that achievement.

After navigating relationship stress, becoming a single dad, and doubling down on education, Kevin earned his MBA and started building systems that combine mindset, structure, and technology for athletes and coaches.

Questions

What coaches usually ask first

Is this 100% online and AI-powered?

Yes. IgniteGrapple is a digital platform. You film practice/matches, upload, and the AI handles the first layer of breakdown. You approve, adjust, and decide what goes to your athletes.

Do I need special cameras or software?

No. Your current phone or tablet is enough. This is built to fit into real-life wrestling rooms, not polished studio setups.

Will this replace real coaching?

No. IgniteGrapple is built to make good coaches more dangerous — by reducing the time spent on repetitive breakdowns and admin work so you can coach live.

What does “beta” actually mean?

Beta means the product is actively being built with early partners. You get discounted pricing, direct access to the builder, influence on feature priorities, and a chance to shape how this looks for wrestling long-term.

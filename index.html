Workflow

How IgniteGrapple fits into your week

No new complicated tech stack. Just what you already use: your phone, your mat, and your athletes.

  1. 1
    Record what matters.
    Use your phone to film live goes, specific drills, or matches. You don’t need perfect camera angles – IgniteGrapple is built for real-life gyms.
  2. 2
    Upload to IgniteGrapple.
    Drop clips into your coach dashboard, tag the athlete and practice type, and let the AI do the first pass of the breakdown.
  3. 3
    Review the AI notes.
    See patterns: slow setups, poor head position, weak mat returns, riding time issues, and common escape mistakes. Edit or add your own comments in seconds.
  4. 4
    Turn it into a plan.
    Auto-generate position-based practice plans or weekly focuses for each athlete or group – complete with drill ideas and live scenarios.
  5. 5
    Send clear progress updates.
    Export a one-page summary you can send to athletes and parents so everyone understands the work you’re doing and where they’re improving.

Questions

What coaches usually ask first

Is this 100% online and AI-powered?

Yes. IgniteGrapple is a digital platform. You film on your phone, upload, and the AI handles the first layer of breakdown. You stay in control of what you agree with and what you send to your athletes.

Do I need special cameras or software?

No. Your current phone or tablet is enough. The goal is to plug into what you already do, not force you into new hardware or complicated editing tools.

Will this replace real coaching?

Never. IgniteGrapple is built to make good coaches more dangerous – by taking the repetitive breakdown and paperwork off your plate so you can focus on mentoring, culture, and live coaching.

How does data & privacy work?

We treat your room’s film like it belongs to you – because it does. Beta partners will get a simple agreement covering how clips are stored, who can see what, and how to remove data if you ever leave.
